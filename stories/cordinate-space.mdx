## Standard CSSOM Coordinate Systems
1. Offset: 
 - Origin: Top-left corner of the element where the event occurred.
 - Example: MouseEvent.offsetX and MouseEvent.offsetY give the coordinates relative to the element receiving the event.

2. Viewport (client):
- Origin: Top-left corner of Viewport
- Example: MouseEvent.clientX and MouseEvent.clientY give the mouse position relative to the viewport.

3. page
- Origin: Top-left corner of entire document
- MouseEvent.pageX and MouseEvent.pageY remain constant for the same position in the document, even if scrolled.

4. Screen:
- Origin: Top-left corner of the user's screen.
- Example: MouseEvent.screenX and MouseEvent.screenY provide coordinates relative to the screen.
